This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information when you visit our website and use our services (including viewing our portfolio, booking studio space, and contacting us). If you do not agree with this policy, please refrain from using the site.

Information we collect

  • Contact details (e.g., name, email, phone) when you submit forms or inquiries.
  • Booking details (e.g., date, time, selected space, preferences).
  • Payment information if purchases are processed (handled by secure third‑party processors; we do not store full card details).
  • Usage data (pages viewed, device/browser info, approximate location) for performance and analytics.
  • Media submissions you choose to share (e.g., references or project notes).

How we use your information

  • Provide and manage bookings, studio access, and customer support.
  • Improve site performance, content relevance, and user experience.
  • Send service communications (confirmations, reminders, updates).
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent misuse or fraud.
  • With consent, send occasional marketing about new spaces or offers (you can opt out at any time).

Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

We process personal data under one or more of the following bases: performance of a contract (e.g., your booking), legitimate interests (e.g., site security, service improvement), compliance with a legal obligation, and consent (e.g., optional marketing).

Cookies & similar technologies

We use essential cookies for core functionality and optional cookies for analytics and performance. You can control cookies via your browser settings. If we present a cookie banner, your choices will be honored.

Analytics

We may use analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, privacy‑respecting alternatives) to understand traffic and usage trends. These tools collect information sent by your device or browser and may set their own cookies.

When we share information

  • Service providers acting on our behalf (hosting, storage/CDN, analytics, payment processing, email).
  • Legal or regulatory authorities when required by law or to protect rights and safety.
  • Business transfers (e.g., merger, acquisition) where data may be part of the assets transferred.

Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, to comply with legal obligations, or to resolve disputes. Retention periods vary by data category and applicable law.

Security

We employ administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data, and to object or withdraw consent. You may also have data portability rights. To exercise these, contact us.

California residents (CCPA/CPRA) and EEA/UK residents (GDPR) may have additional rights. We will honor applicable requests subject to verification and legal exceptions.

Children's privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child provided us with data, please contact us to remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will post the updated version here and revise the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes may be communicated via the site or email when appropriate.

Contact us

For privacy questions or requests, email privacy@yourdomain.com.
