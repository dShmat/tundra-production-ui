Booking & Payments

  • Bookings are confirmed once payment is received. Unpaid holds may be released without notice.
  • Setup and breakdown must be completed within your reserved time.
  • Reschedules/cancellations follow our policy (see your confirmation email). Fees may apply.
  • Commercial projects may require proof of insurance and/or permits.

Access, Check‑In & Hours

  • Standard hours: 09:00–21:00 (custom times by request).
  • Please arrive on time; late arrival does not extend your booking.
  • Check‑in and orientation are required before use of any equipment.
  • Only registered guests are permitted entry; keep doors closed at all times.

Use of Space

  • Protect floors and walls. Use furniture pads and sandbags where appropriate.
  • Do not drill, nail, or permanently alter any surface.
  • Respect neighbors; keep sound at reasonable levels.
  • Cyclorama: shoes clean; no hard wheels; no painting unless pre‑approved.

Equipment

  • Included gear must be used safely and returned to its original condition and location.
  • Only qualified operators may handle electrical, grip, and lighting equipment.
  • Report defects immediately. Do not use damaged gear.
  • Additional rentals are available on request and billed separately.
  • Food and drinks are permitted in designated areas only; keep them away from gear.
  • No smoking or vaping inside the studio.
  • Alcohol requires prior approval; local laws and permits must be observed.

Restricted Items & Activities

  • No weapons, illegal substances, or hazardous materials.
  • No rooftop access or exterior mounting without written permission.
  • Drone use indoors requires trained operator and prior approval.

Cleanliness & Reset

  • Leave the space as you found it: trash in bins, furniture/equipment returned.
  • Excessive mess (including glitter/paint/spills) incurs an additional cleaning fee.
  • Report any damages before departure.

Overtime & Penalties

  • Overtime is billed in 30‑minute increments. Exiting the building must occur before your end time.
  • Unauthorized overtime or early access will be charged at the posted rate or higher.
  • Repeated violations may result in refusal of future bookings.

Damages & Liability

  • You are responsible for all persons in your party and any damage during your booking.
  • Fees apply for repainting the cyclorama, repairs, or replacement of damaged gear.
  • The studio is not liable for personal property lost, stolen, or left behind.

Privacy & Security

  • CCTV may be in operation in common areas for safety and security.
  • Respect other clients’ privacy; filming outside your reserved area is prohibited.
  • Personal data is handled according to our Privacy Policy.

Contact

Questions about these rules? Contact us at hello@yourdomain.com.
